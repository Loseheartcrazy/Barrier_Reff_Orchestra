CP1406 - 2017 Group Project Plan

Group Number 130

Members: Xiezheng Zhang 13119805, Tianshi Liang 12795222

Mission Statement

  • Raise awareness of the Barrier Reef Orchestra.
  • Increase the number of people attending the concerts and getting involved in the Orchestra itself.
  • Build a clear and useful user interface and layout to replace the old website, and keep the original document and information.
  • Develop the theme color as the client requirement "coral reef and sea".
  • Use the modern style to furniture the website. Guide users to the page which they want to, and make them dismiss the next concert information.
  • Increase the number of sponsors for the orchestra

Success Evaluation

  • A count of the amount of tickets sold via the Civic Theatre, or via calling.
  • An increase in the number of benefactors.
  • An increase in the number of applicants to be players in the orchestra will be counted and compared to previous months to measure any potential increase.
  • The feedbacks received from audiences will be utilised and analysed in regards to what people want from the orchestra.

Target Audience

  • The site will be designed to appeal to a mid age demographic of people (both genders), in particular, ages between 20 - 50.
  • To attract people from this age group the site will have a simple content and structure.
  • It will be easy to identify where information can be found. It is also included interactive methods like hiding text and being able to view the text by pressing a button that says more.
  • It will not have large amounts of texts that may be hard to read. Various images that will make the site look attractive and spunky for the targeted comstomer group.
